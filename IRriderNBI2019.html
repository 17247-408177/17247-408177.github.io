Backstage

Dressing Rooms

Injury Reserve will need (1) dressing room for the sole use of Injury Reserve (NO OPENING ACTS - NO PRESS). No person other than Injiury Reserve and crew shall have access to the dressing room without the prior permission of Artist Manager. Please plan to leave the dressing rooms open at least (60) minutes after loadout.

Room should be lockable, clean, well-lit, air-conditioned, and in close proximity to the stage. Please make sure hot & cold water is working, hand soap, a full-length mirror, private bathroom, power strips, empty trash cans, a cooler/fridge, and comfortably furnished.

Production Office

Please provide a clean, well lit, quiet production office (preferably away from the stage) with table, desk chair, working internet, and access to printer.
This room should be locked at all times. The keys are to be given to Artist & Tour Manager upon the time of arrival at the venue.

Meals

If you are not providing a catered meal, please have a "menu book" available in the Production Office with lunch and dinner options.
Please note there are Vegetarians and Vegans in the touring party and need to have equal options avialable.
Buyout to be provided in Cash upon arrival if a hot meal is not available.
